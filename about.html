professional info

I'm an LGBTQIA+ digital designer with 10 years of experience in Minecraft mapmaking. I've been doing work for the Minecraft Marketplace for about 4 years. I've worked with groups such as Tinkrew, Spark Universe, Team Vaeron, Sapphire Studios, and 57Digital. I have a huge passion for creating meaningful digital experiences. in 2019, I was a member of the Wynncraft content production team, designing questing environments. in 2020, I worked with Open Pit Presents, a group of artists and activists dedicated to creating digital events for music enthusiasts to gather in virtual venues, listening to their favorite artists perform to raise funds for charity organizations. in my time there, we hosted events such as NETHER MEANT, SQUARE GARDEN, STRING FORMAL, AETH3R, and LAVAPALOOZA.
